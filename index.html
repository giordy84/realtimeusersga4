Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

Built for website owners, marketers, and developers who want a fast, clean window into their live traffic.

🔐

Your data, your account

Sign in with your own Google account. The app reads directly from your GA4 property — we never store or see your analytics data.

📊

Minute-by-minute chart

A bar chart showing user activity for each of the last 30 minutes, refreshed automatically on your chosen interval.

⚡

Smart quota protection

The app monitors your GA4 API quota and automatically slows the refresh rate if it runs low — no manual intervention needed.

🎨

Themes & layouts

Five color themes, three dashboard layouts, and six fonts. Make the app feel like yours.

🏠

Multiple properties

All GA4 properties linked to your Google account are available. Switch between them in one tap.

🌙

Dark & light mode

Follows your system preference or lets you choose manually. Looks great on any device, day or night.
